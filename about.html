OUR ORIGINS

The club was established in 1984 by Sensei Ian Garside 5th Dan KUGB/JKA. The club is now run by committee with the senior instructor being Sensei Stephen Warburton 4th Dan KUGB. We operate two dojoâ€™s in the Rossendale area of Lancashire in the UK.



Dojo - Haslingden

Our Headquarters are based at Haslingden Primary School.

Training times at this Dojo are:

  • Sunday morning 10am to 11:30am (For ALL grades) and 11:30pm to 12pm (Brown and Black belts)
  • Tuesday evenings 6:30pm to 8pm (For ALL grades) and 8pm to 8:30pm (Brown and Black belts)

PARKING: The car park and entrance is on East Bank Avenue opposite the Haslingden Swimming Baths.


Dojo - Bacup

We also use the AB&D Centre in Bacup. This is located next to the Post Office in the Centre of Bacup as you head up Burnley Road.

Training takes place on the second floor in the Dance Studio. The entrance is at the side, across from the Post Office.

PLEASE NOTE: If the door is locked, ring the bell marked Karate.

Training times at this Dojo are:

  • Thursday evenings 6:15pm to 7:45pm (Beginners to Brown belt) and 8pm to 9pm (Brown and Black belts)

PARKING: On the main road beside the shops, opposite the building.



Membership

Your first 3 lessons are free, however; if Karate is for you and you wish to become a full time member you will need to apply for an annual license to continue with your training.
